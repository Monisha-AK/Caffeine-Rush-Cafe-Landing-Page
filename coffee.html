About The Coffee

South Indian Filter Coffee

A rich, slow-brewed coffee made by dripping hot water through a blend of roasted coffee beans and chicory. The resulting thick decoction is vigorously mixed with hot milk and sugar, traditionally poured back and forth between two metal cups to create a signature frothy layer.

₹ 80

Espresso

A strong, concentrated coffee drink pulled in small espresso cups. It's the foundation for almost every drink on this menu — lattes, cappuccinos, and macchiatos all start here.

₹ 110

Americano

Equal parts espresso and hot water, enjoyed black or with a touch of cream. The added water softens the intensity without losing the espresso's character.

₹ 130

Cappuccino

Known for its creamy texture and balanced flavour, made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam on top. Finished with a light dusting of cocoa.

₹ 150

Latte

Steamed milk and espresso with a thin layer of foam on top. Known for its mild, creamy taste, it can be served hot or iced and pairs well with flavoured syrups.

₹ 160

Flat White

A double shot of espresso under a thin layer of velvety steamed milk. Stronger and more coffee-forward than a latte, with far less foam standing in the way.

₹ 170

Mocha

Chocolatey, sweet, and indulgent. Made with steamed milk, real chocolate syrup, and espresso, topped with a final layer of foam for a dessert-like finish.

₹ 180

Macchiato

Espresso "stained" with just a spoonful of foamed milk — the name literally means marked. Small in size but big in intensity, built for purists.

₹ 140

Irish Coffee

Hot coffee sweetened with brown sugar and lifted with a measure of Irish whiskey, finished with a float of lightly whipped cream on top. Warm, bold, and a little indulgent.

₹ 260

Cold Brew

Coarse-ground coffee steeped in cold water for 18 hours, never touched by heat. The result is smooth, naturally sweet, and noticeably lower in acidity than hot-brewed coffee.

₹ 190

Iced Latte

Espresso poured over cold milk and ice — all the mildness of a latte, served cold for warmer days. Customisable with vanilla, caramel, or hazelnut syrup.

₹ 180

Frappé

An iced coffee drink made by blending instant coffee, ice, sugar, and water until thick and frothy. Milk, cream, or even ice cream can be added for an extra-creamy finish.

₹ 210

Affogato

A scoop of vanilla gelato "drowned" in a hot shot of espresso, eaten with a spoon as the two slowly melt into each other. Half dessert, half coffee, entirely worth it.

₹ 220

Hazelnut Cappuccino

Velvety microfoam, double-shot espresso, and a house-made hazelnut syrup with a dusting of cacao.

₹ 160

Cardamom Cold Brew

18-hour cold brew steeped with green cardamom pods, served over ice with a splash of coconut cream.

₹ 200

Brown Sugar Latte

Espresso shaken with brown sugar syrup, oat milk, and a hint of cinnamon — sweet, smoky, and dangerously smooth.

₹ 180
